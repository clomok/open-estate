Personal FAQ

Where are the physical documents?

The original Trust documents are located in the fireproof safe in the master closet. The combination is in the "Details" section under the Trustor's profile.

Who is the primary contact at the Bank?

Contact the Branch Manager at the main local branch. They should have a copy of the Certificate of Trust on file.

What do we do immediately upon passing?

  1. Notify the Financial Advisor (details in "Assets").
  2. Order 10 copies of the death certificate.
  3. Contact the Estate Attorney (details in the Sidebar/Contacts).
